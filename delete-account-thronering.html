Delete Account and Data

Throne Ring - Agon-inspired royal formation
Effective Date: April 18, 2026  |  Last Updated: April 18, 2026

What This Page Is For

Use this page to understand how to request deletion of account-linked data for Throne Ring. Offline-only progress stored only on your device can be removed by deleting the app or clearing app data on your device.

Data Covered By Deletion

  • Firebase account identifiers and linked cloud save data for Throne Ring.
  • Game progress, tutorial completion, ratings, hint balance, achievements mirrored in the cloud, and settings associated with your account.
  • Support request metadata needed to verify and complete the deletion request.

Data Not Deleted By This Request

  • Purchase records managed by Apple, Google, or RevenueCat, which may be retained for tax, fraud prevention, refund, and legal reasons.
  • Aggregated analytics that no longer identify your account.
  • Crash logs already separated from account identity.

How To Request Deletion

  1. Open Throne Ring and sign in or link the account you want deleted, if account features are enabled.
  2. Use the in-app support or store support email and include the game name, your account email if linked, and the phrase: Delete my Throne Ring account data.
  3. We will verify the request and delete eligible account-linked data within the required platform policy window.
Do not send passwords or payment card details in deletion requests.

After Deletion

Cloud progress, synced settings, and account-linked gameplay data for Throne Ring will no longer be recoverable. You may continue playing offline or create a new account later.
