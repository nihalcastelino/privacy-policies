🛡 Introduction

Welcome to Space Adventure ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our website and in using our services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, use our mobile app, and play our games.

By using Space Adventure, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

💾 Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, display name, and password when you create an account
  • Game Data: Game progress, scores, achievements, ship inventory, and gameplay statistics
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Stripe (we never store credit card details)
  • Profile Information: Avatar, preferences, and settings you choose to share

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type, and screen resolution
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, game sessions, and interactions
  • Location Data: Country/region (for regional pricing and GDPR compliance)
  • IP Address: Used for security, fraud prevention, and regional detection

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

  • Essential Cookies: Required for website functionality (session management, security)
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by Google AdSense to show personalized ads (requires consent)
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our website
  • Personalization Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings

👁 How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our gaming services
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Personalize your gaming experience and show relevant content
  • Send you important updates, security alerts, and support messages
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our games and services
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and ensure platform security
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms
  • Show personalized advertisements (with your consent)

🔒 Data Storage & Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Supabase: User accounts, game data, and preferences are stored securely in Supabase (encrypted at rest and in transit)
  • Stripe: Payment information is processed by Stripe (PCI-DSS compliant; we never store credit card details)
  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular Audits: Regular security assessments and updates

👤 Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), UK, Switzerland, or California, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your data for certain purposes
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for cookie usage at any time
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt-out of sale of personal information (California residents)

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email address below.

🚸 Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13 (or 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

🌍 International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including:

🗑 Account & Data Deletion Policy

You have the right to delete your account and all associated personal data at any time. We provide multiple ways to request account deletion:

Option 1 — In-App Deletion

  1. Open Space Adventure and sign in to your account
  2. Navigate to your Profile (tap your avatar or the profile icon)
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted
  5. Your account and all associated data will be queued for permanent deletion

Option 2 — Email Request

Send an email to privacy@spacerace.games with the subject line "Account Deletion Request". Include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 30 days.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Account credentials — email, display name, authentication tokens
  • Game data — progress, scores, achievements, leaderboard entries
  • Purchase history — in-app coin balances and ship inventory
  • Profile information — avatar, preferences, settings
  • Multiplayer data — game room history, online match records
  • Cookie preferences — consent settings and personalization data

What May Be Retained

  • Transaction records — Stripe payment records may be retained for legal/financial compliance (typically 7 years)
  • Anonymized analytics — Aggregated, non-identifiable usage data may be retained for service improvement
  • Legal obligations — Data required by law to be retained (e.g., fraud prevention records)

Deletion Timeline

In-app deletion: Account is deactivated immediately. All personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days.

Email requests: We will acknowledge your request within 7 days and complete deletion within 30 days of verification.

Important Notes

  • Account deletion is permanent and irreversible — your game progress, coins, ships, and achievements cannot be recovered
  • Active premium subscriptions should be cancelled before deletion to avoid future charges
  • If you used Google or Apple sign-in, deleting your Space Adventure account does not affect your Google/Apple account
