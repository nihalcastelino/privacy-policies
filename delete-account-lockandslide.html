Delete Account & Data

Lock & Slide

📄

Overview

Lock & Slide uses Google Play Games (Android) or Apple Game Center (iOS) for sign-in. We do not maintain separate user accounts — your identity is managed entirely by Google or Apple.

This page explains how to delete all data associated with your Lock & Slide account and gameplay.

📱

Step 1 — Delete Local Data

All gameplay progress, settings, streaks, and preferences are stored locally on your device. To delete this data:

1
Android Settings → Apps → Lock & Slide → Storage → Clear Data
1
iOS Delete (uninstall) the app. All local data is removed automatically.
This immediately removes all local progress, crown ratings, daily streak history, and settings.
☁️

Step 2 — Delete Cloud Save Data

If you used the Cloud Save feature (via Google Play Games), your progress was backed up to Google's servers. To delete it:

1
Open Lock & Slide Go to Settings → Account
2
Delete Cloud Save Tap "Delete Cloud Data" to remove your saved game from Google Play Games

Alternatively, you can manage your Play Games data directly:

•
Google Play Games Open the Play Games app → Profile → Settings → Delete Play Games account & data
•
Apple Game Center Game Center data can be managed in iOS Settings → Game Center
📊

Step 3 — Analytics & Crash Data

Lock & Slide uses Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics, which collect anonymized usage and crash data. This data:

  • Is not tied to a personal identity
  • Cannot be individually retrieved or deleted
  • Is automatically purged by Google Firebase according to their retention policies (typically 60 days for crash data, 14 months for analytics)

To stop future collection, simply uninstall the app.

💰

In-App Purchases

Your "Remove Ads" purchase is managed by the Google Play Store or Apple App Store, not by us. Deleting your game data does not affect your purchase record.

If you reinstall the app, you can restore your purchase via Settings → Purchases → Restore.
⚠️

What Gets Deleted

This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

When you complete all steps above, the following data is permanently removed:

  • All puzzle completion records and crown ratings
  • Daily challenge streak history
  • Endless mode statistics
  • App settings and preferences
  • Cloud save backup (if applicable)

The following is not deleted (managed by platform):

  • Your Google or Apple account
  • In-app purchase records
  • Leaderboard scores (managed by Play Games / Game Center)
