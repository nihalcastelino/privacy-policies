Account & Data Deletion

Space Adventure

Effective Date: April 10, 2026  •  Last Updated: April 10, 2026

🛡 Overview

At Space Adventure, we respect your right to control your personal data. You may request the deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. This page explains how to delete your account, what data is affected, and the timeline for processing your request.

⚠ Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once processed, your game progress, coins, ships, achievements, and all personal data cannot be recovered.

📱 How to Delete Your Account

Option 1 — In-App Deletion

  1. Open Space Adventure and sign in to your account
  2. Tap your avatar or the profile icon to open your Profile
  3. Scroll down to the bottom of your profile
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
  5. Read the confirmation dialog and tap "Yes, Delete My Account"
  6. Your account is immediately deactivated and queued for permanent deletion

Option 2 — Email Request

  1. Send an email to privacy@spacerace.games
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include the email address associated with your Space Adventure account
  4. We will verify your identity and confirm receipt within 7 days
  5. Deletion will be completed within 30 days of verification

⏱ Deletion Timeline

Immediate (in-app deletion): Your account is deactivated instantly. You are signed out and can no longer access your account.

Within 7 days (email requests): We acknowledge your request and verify your identity.

Within 30 days: All personal data marked for deletion is permanently removed from our systems, including backups.

If your request cannot be processed (e.g., we cannot verify your identity), we will notify you and explain the reason.

ℹ Before You Delete

Please review the following before proceeding with account deletion:

  • Cancel active subscriptions: If you have a premium subscription, cancel it first to avoid future charges. Deleting your account does not automatically cancel subscriptions managed by app stores.
  • Spend remaining coins: Your coin balance and purchased ships will be permanently lost and cannot be refunded.
  • No recovery: Once deletion is complete, there is no way to restore your account, progress, or purchases. You would need to create a new account to play again.
  • Third-party sign-in: If you signed in with Google or Apple, deleting your Space Adventure account does not affect your Google or Apple account.
  • Shared devices: If you play on multiple devices, you will be signed out on all devices.

⚖ Your Rights

Under GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable privacy laws, you also have the right to:

  • Access a copy of your personal data before deletion
  • Export your data in a portable format (data portability)
  • Restrict processing instead of full deletion
  • Object to specific types of data processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below. We will respond within 30 days as required by law.
