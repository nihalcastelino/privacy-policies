Privacy Policy

Aqua Sort - Water sort puzzle
Effective Date: April 19, 2026  |  Last Updated: April 19, 2026

Overview

Aqua Sort is a mobile-first puzzle game from FluffyLlama.Games. This policy explains what data may be collected when you play, sync progress, view ads, make purchases, or use platform achievements.

Data We May Collect

  • Gameplay progress, ratings, tutorial completion, hint balances, achievements, and settings.
  • Anonymous or linked account identifiers if you choose cloud save through Firebase.
  • Purchase and entitlement status through RevenueCat.
  • Crash logs, diagnostics, app version, device type, and privacy-safe analytics events.
  • Advertising identifiers and consent state when AdMob ads are enabled and permitted.

How We Use Data

  • Save progress locally and, if you opt in, sync it across devices.
  • Validate purchases, restore entitlements, and remove ads for eligible players.
  • Operate daily challenges, achievements, tutorial progress, and game balancing.
  • Find crashes, improve performance, and protect game integrity.

Third-Party Services

Aqua Sort may use Firebase, RevenueCat, Google AdMob, Google Play Games Services, and Apple Game Center. These services process data under their own policies and are integrated only for app functionality, purchases, ads, analytics, crash reporting, cloud save, and achievements.

Children and Families

Aqua Sort is designed as a general audience puzzle game. We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information, precise location, contacts, photos, or message content.

Your Choices

  • You can play core content offline without cloud save.
  • You can decline ad tracking where platform consent prompts are shown.
  • You can request deletion of account-linked data using the delete account page for this game.
Delete account page: delete-account-aquasort.html

Contact

For privacy requests, contact FluffyLlama.Games through the support channel listed in the app store page for Aqua Sort.
