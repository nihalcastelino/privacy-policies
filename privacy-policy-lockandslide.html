Welcome

Lock & Slide ("the App") is a word puzzle game developed by FluffyLlama.Games ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

By using Lock & Slide, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

📊

Information We Collect

We collect limited data to improve your experience and maintain the App:

Analytics Data (Firebase Analytics)

  • Gameplay events: puzzle starts, completions, crown ratings, and difficulty levels
  • App usage patterns: screen views, session duration, and feature engagement
  • Device information: device model, operating system version, and app version

Crash Reports (Firebase Crashlytics)

  • Crash logs, stack traces, and device state at the time of a crash
  • This data helps us identify and fix bugs to keep the App running smoothly
We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, photos, contacts, or any other personally identifiable information.
📰

Advertising

Lock & Slide displays ads through Google AdMob to support free access to the game. AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA on iOS, Advertising ID on Android)
  • General device and interaction data to serve relevant ads

iOS Users: You will be asked for permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework before any tracking occurs. You can deny this request and still use the App fully.

Remove Ads: You can purchase the "Remove Ads" upgrade within the App to permanently disable all advertising. No ad-related data will be collected after this purchase.

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

💰

In-App Purchases

Lock & Slide offers a one-time "Remove Ads" purchase. All transactions are processed by the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not directly collect or store any payment information.

Purchase status is stored locally on your device to remember your ad-free preference.

🔒

Data Storage & Security

All gameplay progress, settings, and streak data are stored locally on your device using standard platform storage. We do not maintain user accounts or cloud databases.

Analytics and crash data are processed by Google Firebase under their security standards. We retain this data only as long as necessary to improve the App.

👥

Children's Privacy

Lock & Slide is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

Ads shown in the App are not personalized for users who have not consented to tracking.

🌐

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics
  • Google AdMob
  • Google Play Services
  • Apple App Store
⚙️

Your Choices

  • Opt out of personalized ads: Deny the ATT prompt on iOS, or reset your Advertising ID on Android via Settings > Privacy > Ads
  • Remove ads entirely: Purchase the "Remove Ads" upgrade in Settings
  • Delete local data: Uninstall the App to remove all locally stored data
  • Opt out of analytics: Disable usage data sharing in your device settings
